DriveSafe Hub - Site Planning Document

Site Name

DriveSafe Hub: A comprehensive portal for understanding driver's license procedures, vehicle registration, and document renewals in Nigeria. Chosen for its simplicity, relevance, and strong branding potential.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a guide for individuals needing information and assistance with obtaining a driver’s license, registering a vehicle, and renewing vehicle documents. It simplifies processes, provides step-by-step instructions, and links users to necessary government platforms.

Scenarios

  • How do I apply for a new driver’s license and what documents do I need?
  • What’s the process to renew my expired vehicle documents?

Color Schema

  • #003049 (Dark Blue) - Used for headers and primary navigation
  • #F77F00 (Orange) - Used for CTAs, links, and buttons

Typography

  • Montserrat - Used for headings
  • Roboto - Used for body text and general content
